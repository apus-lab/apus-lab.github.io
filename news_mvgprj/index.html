AIAAJ - Modal Analysis via Multivariate Gaussian Process Regression

Published: Jan 20, 2025 by Daning Huang

Our ArXiv manuscript has just been published on AIAAJ, led by our lab member, Jiwoo Song. This is an innovative application of multi-variate Gaussian process regression to the modal analysis of spatiotemporal data, such as unsteady flow fields. Our method is particularly suitable for the cases where the data set is scarce and/or the temporal sampling is irregular; in these cases, the conventional methods such as spectral proper orthogonal decomposition do not work well. This is one of our efforts in one of our core thrusts, Scientific Data Analysis.
