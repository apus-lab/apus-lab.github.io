Research contract awarded from Sandia NL

Fluid-thermal-structural interaction and shear lay instability

Published: Dec 7, 2022 by Daning Huang

Together with researchers from the Sandia National Labs, we will further our previous study on the high-speed fluid-thermal-structural interaction problem on a complex geometry, with a goal to elucidate the major instability mechanisms in this coupled dynamical system.
