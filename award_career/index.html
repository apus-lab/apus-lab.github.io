Winning NSF CAREER Award 2024

Reduced-Order Modeling, Control Co-Design, Morphing Vehicles

Published: Apr 2, 2024 by Daning Huang

Supported by the NSF, we will develop “Physics-Infused Reduced-Order Modeling for Control Co-Design of Morphing Aerial Autonomous Systems”. This enables us to pursue our four research thrusts in a unified manner: integrate ROMs and control & autonomy to achieve the full-stack design optimization of an engineering system.
