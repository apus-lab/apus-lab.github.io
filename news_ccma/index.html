Poster at PSU CCMA 2023

Weaker Form for Learning Nonlinear Dynamical Systems

Published: Nov 13, 2023 by Daning Huang

The symposium on computational mathematics for engineering and sciences, held by CCMA, took place on Nov. 13-14. We contributed a poster on learning algorithms for dynamical systems, that achieve high accuracy with low computational cost and, most importantly, are easy to implement. This is a combination of work from our Ph.D. students, Yin Yu and Carlos Vargas Venegas.
