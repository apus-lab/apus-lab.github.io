APUS at SSDM 2025

Aeroservoelastic modeling + Trajectory optimization

Published: May 7, 2025 by Daning Huang

At the recent ASME Aerospace Structures, Structural Dynamics, and Materials Conference (SSDM) 2025 conference, we presented our on-going work towards the control co-design of morphing aerial vehicles, supported by our NSF CAREER Award.

In this presentation, we focused on the mid-fidelity aeroservoelastic modeling, as well as the trajectory optimization (TO) based on this model. In particular, we explored sampling-based algorithms to solve the aeroservoelastic TO problem, which would have been computationally intractable if the standard gradient-based algorithms were used.

For more details, see paper and slides.
