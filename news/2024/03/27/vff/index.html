Congrats to Mr. Jiwoo Song for winning the VFF Scholarship!

Published: Mar 27, 2024 by Daning Huang

The Vertical Flight Foundation (VFF) Scholarship, administered by the Vertical Flight Society, is awarded every year to the world’s most talented engineering students interested in vertical flight. Since 1977, nearly 700 scholarships have been awarded.

This year, Jiwoo is among the 12 recipients in the Masters’ category. This marks a good start of his journey of Ph.D. to rotorcraft blade optimization. Congratulations!

Also, among the total of 29 VFF scholarships (Bachelor, Master’s, and Ph.D.), five are Penn State students. We Are!
