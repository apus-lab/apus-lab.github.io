Dr. Huang delivered a research seminar at RPI

Geometry-Informed Reduced-Order Modeling Towards Control Co-Design of Complex Dynamical Systems

Published: Oct 1, 2025 by Daning Huang

On Oct. 1st, Dr. Huang delivered a seminar in Department of Mechanical, Aerospace, and Nuclear Engineering at Rensselear Polytechnic Institute. The seminar is invited by Prof. Shaowu Pan at RPI. The seminar presents some of the key achievements of the group in the past years, esp. Impossible Trinity of Modeling, and some of the current main research thrusts.

It was a fun visit.
